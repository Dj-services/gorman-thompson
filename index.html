Our Story

From one flooring job to a full-service crew.

Gorman Thompson Construction LLC was founded in Smithville, Missouri by Nate Thompson, who built the company's name on flooring installation and interior painting before expanding into full home renovations. Nate still leads every project personally, which is why the same care that went into that first LVP install shows up in every job since.

We're a small, local operation on purpose. Fewer projects running at once means more attention on yours — clear communication, honest pricing, and a crew that shows up when they say they will.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — every estimate is free and comes with no obligation. We'll walk the project with you and follow up with a written quote, usually within 24 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Smithville, MO and serve homeowners throughout the surrounding Northland area of Kansas City. Not sure if you're in range? Just give us a call.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Gorman Thompson Construction LLC is licensed and insured, so you're covered from the first day of the project to the last.

How long does a typical flooring or painting job take?

It depends on scope — a single room can often be done in a day or two, while full-home flooring or exterior painting may take a week or more. We'll give you a real timeline with your quote.

Do you subcontract the work out?

No. The same crew that quotes your job is the one that shows up to do it, which is why our clients know exactly who's in their home.
